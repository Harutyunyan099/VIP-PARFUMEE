Philosophy

Perfume is the most intimate form of memory

Sillage — the French word for the scented trail a person leaves behind. It is what remains of you in a room after you have gone. We believe choosing a fragrance is choosing how you will be remembered.

Every bottle in this collection was selected by hand — icons that defined eras, and quiet masterpieces known only to those who seek them.
